Spicy Vodka Pasta

If you are ever craving a delicious pasta dish, this will be the quickest and easiest recipe you will find.

"Simple yet quite delicious!"

Ingredients

    • ¼ of olive oil
    • 1 clove of garlic
    • ¼ onion
    • ¼ cup of tomato paste
    • ½ cup of heavy cream
    • 1 tablespoon of vodka
    • 1 teaspoon of red pepper flakes
    • 225 grams of shells pasta
    • 1 tablespoon of butter
    • Choppped basil
    • ½ cup of parmesan cheese
    • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

    1. In a large pan, heat olive oil, garlic and onion. Cook until soft, then add the tomato paste and cook until it appears darker.
    2. Add in the heavy cream and the vodka. Cook through until evaporated.
    3. Add the red pepper flakes, and if you want it to be extra spicy add more.
    4. Season with salt and pepper and stir until combined. Once it's well combined, remove the sauce from the heat.
    5. Add water in a pot and cook the pasta for 10-15 minutes. When the pasta is done, save a ¼ cup of the water, and drain the pasta.
    6. Return the sauce to the heat, and add butter to it. Stir until melted.
    7. Add the pasta water and the past to the sauce, as well as the parmesan cheese. Stir until it's all combined.
    8. Serve your pasta and sprinkle parmesan cheese on top, and if you want, chop some basil and place it on top.
    9. Add more parmesan cheese, and enjoy your delicious Spicy Vodka Pasta.
